Movie Recommendations

3 classic horror movies to watch

Island of lost Souls (1932)

A very classic horror movie about a sailor who becomes stranded on an island where a mad scientist, Dr. Monreau is creating monstrous hybrids of man and beast. It's a really old movie with a lot of really dramatic shots, which make it hilarious.

Memorable qoutes from the movie:
"Are we not men?!"
"You made us in the house of pain!"
"He tell me spill blood."

28 days Later (2002)

A gritty and frantic story of survival as a man wakes from a coma, finding himself in an apocalyptic world swarming with those infected with the rage virus. In an attempt to find sanctuary, he must venture across the British countryside, banding together with other survivors along the way. Though in this lawless wasteland, it starts to become apparent there is more to fear than the relentless undead.

Memorable qoutes from the movie:
"Mmmm... they're radiated"
"There is no government."
"World's worse place to get a flat."

Predator (1987)

An action packed horror movie about elite paramilitary rescue team head into the heart of the Guatamalan jungle to extract hostages held by an armed guerrilla rebel outfit. The mission takes a sinister turn as they begin to realize there is something not of this world hiding in the dense forest, and its hunting people.

Memorable qoutes from the movie:
"I ain't got time to bleed."
"Get to the choppah!"
"EAUGH!"
